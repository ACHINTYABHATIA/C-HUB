Fee, Pricing and Discount options

Daily Plan:

  • Price: ₹100 per day
  • Benefits: Unlimited parking for 24 hours within a single day.
  • Exclusive Offer: Enjoy a complimentary coffee or tea at our partnered campus café with each booking!
  • Ideal for: Visitors, short-term campus activities, and occasional parkers.
  • Why Choose This Plan: Experience stress-free parking, quick entry, and exit, and make the most of your campus visit without worrying about parking hassles. Our secure facilities ensure your vehicle's safety, giving you peace of mind throughout the day. Plus, the added bonus of a free beverage lets you relax and unwind after your parking experience.

Monthly Plan:

  • Price: ₹1699 per month
  • Benefits: Unlimited parking for the entire month, convenient for regular campus attendees, students, and faculty members.
  • Special Feature: Priority access during peak hours and exclusive discounts on additional services.
  • Ideal for: Students, faculty, and staff members seeking a long-term parking solution.
  • Why Choose This Plan: Say goodbye to the stress of finding parking every day! Our monthly plan offers you the convenience of unlimited parking, ensuring you can focus on what matters most without worrying about your vehicle. Enjoy the perk of priority access during busy hours, allowing you to breeze in and out effortlessly. Plus, our exclusive discounts sweeten the deal, making your parking experience even more cost-effective.

Hourly Plan:

  • Price: ₹10 per hour
  • Benefits: Flexibility to pay for the exact duration of parking, suitable for short visits, meetings, or quick errands.
  • Special Offer: Get a 15% discount on your next hourly booking after five consecutive bookings!
  • Ideal for: Visitors, short-term meetings, and individuals with unpredictable schedules.
  • Why Choose This Plan: Need to run a quick errand or attend a short meeting? Our hourly plan offers you the ultimate flexibility. Pay only for the time you need, saving you money while ensuring a safe parking spot. Plus, with our special offer, your loyalty is rewarded. After just five bookings, enjoy a fantastic 15% discount on your next parking session, making your future visits even more affordable and convenient.
